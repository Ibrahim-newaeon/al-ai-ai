SEO - Paid Advertising

Search Engine

SEM (Search Engine Marketing) is the form of internet marketing that involves the promotion of websites by increasing their visibility in search engine results pages (SERPs) primarily through paid advertising. (That is the main difference between SEM and SEO)

It is an advertising campaign that targets the behavior of the target when searching for a service or product using the search engine, and it is targeting specific words or phrases so that the text of the advertisement appears when the search is performed using these words or texts.

The process of selecting these words or texts is in proportion to the monthly search volume and words related to the nature of the customer’s work, whether it is a service or product. These campaigns are considered one of the most highly interactive search engine campaigns due to the need of the user.
